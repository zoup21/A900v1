Autopilot MMEL(Master Minimum Equipment List)

  • (Inertial measurement unit failure)
  • (Internal autopilot failure. Autopilot has detected one/ multiple failures)
  • (No accurate GPS fix. GPS not used for navigation)
  • (System voltage critical)
  • (NOT AN ALARM. PC or Visionair failure)
  • (LCL)



PCM PRO MMEL(Master Minimum Equipment List)

  • OR (One or more servos has abnormal power consumption, too low or too high)



Engine Control Unit MMEL(Master Minimum Equipment List)

  • (Injector Failure)
  • (Ignition powered off)
  • (Throttle Servo Fail)
  • (Crankshaft Position Sensor fail)
  • (Throttle Position Sign fail)
  • (Too low pressure on fuel pump)


    IMU

    (Inertial measurement unit failure)

  • CHECK condition of shock-absorbing mounts
  • REPLACE shock-absorbing mounts if necessary
  • CHECK ALPHA 900 airframe, blade tracking, or AP wires
    • IMU Alarm OFF

      GO

    • IMU Alarm still ON

      NO GO

    INT

    (Internal autopilot failure. Autopilot has detected one/ multiple failures)

  • CHECK all connections
  • CHECK power supply
    • INT Alarm OFF

      GO

    • INT Alarm still ON

      NO GO

    GPS

    (No accurate GPS fix. GPS not used for navigation)

  • CHECK GPS antenna connection
  • CHECK GPS antenna has clear view of sky
  • WAIT up to 15 minutes for GPS to acquire fix
    • GPS Alarm OFF

      GO

    • GPS Alarm still ON

      NO GO

    UAV VOLT CRITICAL

    (System voltage critical)

  • If RPMS are low this alarm will be triggered
  • Back up battery reading must be between 14-16.5 V
    • V1 Alarm OFF when RPMS are at flying level

      GO

    • V1 Alarm ON when RPMS are at flying level

      NO GO

    PC FAILURE

    (NOT AN ALARM. PC or Visionair failure)

  • RESTART Laptop/GCase Duo
  • LAUNCH Visionair
    • Laptop/GCase Duo and VISIONAIR functioning

      GO

    LONG COMM LOSS

    (LCL)

  • CHECK UAV / GTRACK antenna connections
  • CHECK UAV / GTRACK power supply
  • CHECK GTRACK antenna correct aiming direction
  • Check Total Bandwidth used (lower video quality if needed)
    • LONG COMM LOSS Alarm OFF

      GO

    • LONG COMM LOSS Alarm still ON

      NO GO

    SERVO FAIL OR SERVO HIGH CURRENT

  • Check Servo connection
  • Check Servo mechanical position and link ensure it is not reaching any mechanical limit.
  • Check absence or jittering or erratic behavior on the servo.
    • ECU COMM FAIL OFF

      GO

    • ECU COMM FAIL OFF

      NO GO

    ECU COMM FAIL

  • Check cable and connectors
  • Power on Visionair while the UAV is powered and check correct gauges reading.
    • ECU COMM FAIL OFF

      GO

    • ECU COMM FAIL ON

      NO GO

    ELECTRICAL SYSTEM

  • Visually check that the PCM PRO is in good condition and that nothing has been damaged.
  • Power off and Power on again the system.
    • ELECTRICAL SYSTEM OFF

      GO

    • ELECTRICAL SYSTEM ON

      NO GO

    INJECTOR

    (Injector Failure)

  • Check connections between injector body and the ECU module
  • Check there is no leakage in the fuel lines
  • Check correct actuation of fuel pumps (Pumps sound and demand fuel when activated)
    • INJECTOR ALARM OFF

      GO

    • INJECTOR ALARM ON

      NO GO

    IGNITION 1&2

    (Ignition powered off)

  • Check connections between both ignition modules and ECU module
  • Check connections between both ignition modules and spark plugs
    • IGNITION ALARM OFF

      GO

    • IGNITION ALARM ON

      NO GO

    THROTTLE SERVO

    (Throttle Servo Fail)

  • Check connections between ECU and throttle servo
    • THROTTLE SERVO ALARM OFF

      GO

    • THROTTLE SERVO ALARM ON

      NO GO

    ENGINE SPEED 1 & 2

    (Crankshaft Position Sensor Fail)

  • Check connection between CPS sensor and ECU is ok
  • Check sensor is correctly oriented facing the crankshaft
  • Check Crankshaft magnets are in their position
    • ENGINE SPEED 1 and 2 ALARMS OFF

      GO

    • ENGINE SPEED 1 or 2 ALARM ON

      NO GO

    TPS

    (Throttle Position Signal fail)

  • Check connection between Throttle servo and ECU
  • Check cable is in good conditions
    • TPS ALARM OFF

      GO

    • TPS ALARM ON

      NO GO

    FUEL STATUS

  • Check there is no leakage in the fuel line pipes
  • Check there are no bubbles in the fuel line pipes
  • Check fuel lines and filters are correctly coupled
  • Check correct connections between the ECU and the fuel pump system
    • FUEL PRESSURE FAIL ALARM OFF

      GO

    • FUEL PRESSURE FAIL ALARM ON

      NO GO
